## Kit Contents

With your kit, you should have the following boards:

| Part                                                     | Quantity |
|----------------------------------------------------------|----------|
| [Raspberry Pi](pi)                                       | 1        |
| [Motor Board](motor-board)                               | 1        |
| [Power Board](power-board)                               | 1        |
| [Servo Assembly](servo-assembly) (Arduino + Servo Hat)   | 1        |
